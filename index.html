PGPost

Automated Podcast Publisher for TikTok

A content management platform that helps podcast creators automatically generate short video clips and publish them to TikTok. Connect your RSS feed, generate clips, and grow your audience.

Features for Podcast Creators

🎙

RSS Feed Integration

Connect your podcast RSS feed. PGPost automatically detects new episodes and extracts audio, metadata, and timestamps.

🎬

Auto Clip Generation

AI-powered short video clips created from your podcast episodes. Vertical format optimized for TikTok and Reels.

🚀

Direct TikTok Publishing

Publish clips directly to your TikTok account via Content Posting API. No manual uploads needed.

📊

Multi-Platform Support

Publish to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and VK simultaneously from a single dashboard.

How It Works

1

Connect Your TikTok Account

Authorize via TikTok Login Kit (OAuth 2.0). PGPost requests only the permissions it needs: profile info and video publishing.

2

Add Your Podcast RSS Feed

Paste your RSS feed URL. PGPost parses episodes, extracts highlights, and generates short video clips automatically.

3

Review and Publish

Preview generated clips, edit captions and hashtags, then publish directly to TikTok with one click.

4

Grow Your Audience

Reach new listeners on TikTok. Track performance and optimize your clips for maximum engagement.

TikTok API Integration

PGPost uses official TikTok Content Posting API with the following scopes:

user.info.basic Identify connected account
video.upload Upload video files to TikTok
video.publish Publish content to creator profile
