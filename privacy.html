Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. General

This Privacy Policy describes how PGPost ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our automated podcast publishing platform at poganoeradio.github.io and our TikTok integration ("the Service").

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0, TikTok provides us with your open_id, display name, and avatar URL. This information is used solely to identify the authorized account for content publishing.

We do not store personal data permanently. Session data is kept only for the duration of the publishing session and is not shared with third parties.

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies on this website.

3. TikTok Integration

PGPost uses the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to identify the authorized TikTok account
  • video.upload — to upload video content to TikTok
  • video.publish — to publish video content directly to the user's TikTok profile

Users retain full ownership of their content. PGPost publishes content only to the account authorized by the user.

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is distributed across multiple platforms including TikTok, Telegram, YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Yandex Music. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs your use of their service.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
