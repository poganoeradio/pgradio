Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. General

This Privacy Policy describes how Poganoe Radio ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our automated content publishing service at poganoeradio.github.io and our TikTok integration ("the Service").

2. Data We Collect

Poganoe Radio does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies on this website.

When you authorize our app via TikTok Login Kit, TikTok provides us with your open_id, display name, and avatar URL. This information is used solely to identify the authorized account for content publishing and is not stored permanently or shared with third parties.

3. TikTok Integration

Our app uses the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to identify the authorized TikTok account
  • video.upload — to upload video content to TikTok
  • video.publish — to publish video content directly to TikTok

All video content published through our service is owned by Poganoe Radio. We only publish to our own account (@poganoeradio).

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is distributed across multiple platforms including TikTok, Telegram, YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Yandex Music. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs your use of their service.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
